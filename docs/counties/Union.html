Union County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

April 14, 2021

Union County is served by the Union County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the departments spending fell by 22%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Union County Health Department rose by 17%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 33%.

In 2010, the Union County Health Department ranked 6 in per-person spending among the 52 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 1.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
