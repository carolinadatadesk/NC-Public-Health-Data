Rockingham County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

April 14, 2021

Rockingham County is served by the Rockingham County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the departments spending fell by 12%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Rockingham County Health Department fell by 3%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 10%.

In 2010, the Rockingham County Health Department ranked 16 in per-person spending among the 52 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 22.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
