Henderson County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

April 14, 2021

Henderson County is served by the Henderson County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the department’s spending fell by 4%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Henderson County Health Department rose by 9%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 12%.

In 2010, the Henderson County Health Department ranked 15 in per-person spending among the 52 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 18.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
