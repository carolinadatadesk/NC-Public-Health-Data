Dare County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

February 17, 2021

Dare County is served by the Dare County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the departments spending fell by 1%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Dare County Health Department rose by 9%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 9%.

In 2010, the Dare County Health Department ranked 45 in per-person spending among the 46 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 46.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
