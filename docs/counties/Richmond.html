Richmond County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

April 14, 2021

Richmond County is served by the Richmond County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the departments spending rose by 11%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Richmond County Health Department fell by 4%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending rose 15%.

In 2010, the Richmond County Health Department ranked 35 in per-person spending among the 52 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 46.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
