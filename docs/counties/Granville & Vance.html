Granville-Vance District Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

May 13, 2021

Granville & Vance counties are served by the Granville-Vance District Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the departments spending fell by 1%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Granville-Vance District Health Department rose by 2%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 2%.

In 2010, the Granville-Vance District Health Department ranked 13 in per-person spending among the 53 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 25.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
