Burke County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

April 14, 2021

Burke County is served by the Burke County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the department’s spending fell by 27%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Burke County Health Department fell by 0%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 27%.

In 2010, the Burke County Health Department ranked 3 in per-person spending among the 52 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 2.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
