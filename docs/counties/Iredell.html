Iredell County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

May 13, 2021

Iredell County is served by the Iredell County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the departments spending fell by 12%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Iredell County Health Department rose by 12%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 21%.

In 2010, the Iredell County Health Department ranked 11 in per-person spending among the 53 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 11.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
