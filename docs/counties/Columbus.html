Columbus County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

May 13, 2021

Columbus County is served by the Columbus County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the department’s spending fell by 43%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Columbus County Health Department fell by 4%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 41%.

In 2010, the Columbus County Health Department ranked 44 in per-person spending among the 53 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 33.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
