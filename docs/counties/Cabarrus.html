Cabarrus County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

May 13, 2021

Cabarrus County is served by the Cabarrus County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the department’s spending rose by 13%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Cabarrus County Health Department rose by 18%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 4%.

In 2010, the Cabarrus County Health Department ranked 39 in per-person spending among the 53 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 44.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
