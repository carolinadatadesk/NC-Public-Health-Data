Harnett County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

February 16, 2021

Harnett County is served by the Harnett County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the department’s spending fell by 28%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Harnett County Health Department rose by 16%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 38%.

In 2010, the Harnett County Health Department ranked 15 in per-person spending among the 46 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 7.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and population increase.
