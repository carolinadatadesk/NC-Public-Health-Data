Martin-Tyrrell-Washington District Health

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

February 17, 2021

Martin, Tyrrell & Washington counties are served by the Martin-Tyrrell-Washington District Health. From 2010 to 2018 the department’s spending fell by 35%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Martin-Tyrrell-Washington District Health fell by 6%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 31%.

In 2010, the Martin-Tyrrell-Washington District Health ranked 44 in per-person spending among the 46 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 44.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
