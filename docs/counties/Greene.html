Greene Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

April 14, 2021

Greene County is served by the Greene Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the departments spending fell by 19%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Greene Health Department fell by 1%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 18%.

In 2010, the Greene Health Department ranked 32 in per-person spending among the 52 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 35.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
