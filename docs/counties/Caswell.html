Caswell County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

May 13, 2021

Caswell County is served by the Caswell County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the departments spending fell by 23%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Caswell County Health Department fell by 4%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 20%.

In 2010, the Caswell County Health Department ranked 47 in per-person spending among the 53 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 46.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
