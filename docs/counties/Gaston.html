Gaston County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

May 13, 2021

Gaston County is served by the Gaston County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the department’s spending fell by 31%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Gaston County Health Department rose by 8%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 36%.

In 2010, the Gaston County Health Department ranked 41 in per-person spending among the 53 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 36.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
