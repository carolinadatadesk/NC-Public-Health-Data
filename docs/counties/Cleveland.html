Cleveland County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

April 14, 2021

Cleveland County is served by the Cleveland County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the department’s spending rose by 2%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Cleveland County Health Department fell by 0%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending rose 3%.

In 2010, the Cleveland County Health Department ranked 45 in per-person spending among the 52 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 49.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
