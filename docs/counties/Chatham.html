Chatham County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

February 17, 2021

Chatham County is served by the Chatham County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the departments spending fell by 9%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Chatham County Health Department rose by 17%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 22%.

In 2010, the Chatham County Health Department ranked 30 in per-person spending among the 46 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 30.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
