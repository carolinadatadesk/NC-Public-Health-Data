Jones County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

February 17, 2021

Jones County is served by the Jones County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the departments spending fell by 41%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Jones County Health Department rose by 0%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 41%.

In 2010, the Jones County Health Department ranked 42 in per-person spending among the 46 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 34.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
