Mecklenburg County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

May 13, 2021

Mecklenburg County is served by the Mecklenburg County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the departments spending rose by 65%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Mecklenburg County Health Department rose by 18%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending rose 39%.

In 2010, the Mecklenburg County Health Department ranked 4 in per-person spending among the 53 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 28.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and the change in population.
