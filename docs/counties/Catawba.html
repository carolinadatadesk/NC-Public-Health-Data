Catawba County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

February 16, 2021

Catawba County is served by the Catawba County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the department’s spending fell by 42%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Catawba County Health Department rose by 3%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 43%.

In 2010, the Catawba County Health Department ranked 27 in per-person spending among the 46 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 13.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and population increase.
