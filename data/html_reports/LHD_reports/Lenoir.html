Lenoir County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

February 16, 2021

Lenoir County is served by the Lenoir County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the departments spending fell by 6%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Lenoir County Health Department fell by 4%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 2%.

In 2010, the Lenoir County Health Department ranked 9 in per-person spending among the 46 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 20.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and population increase.
