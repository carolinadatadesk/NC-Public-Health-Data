Greene Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

February 16, 2021

Greene County is served by the Greene Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the departments spending fell by 19%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Greene Health Department fell by 2%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 18%.

In 2010, the Greene Health Department ranked 28 in per-person spending among the 46 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 31.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and population increase.
