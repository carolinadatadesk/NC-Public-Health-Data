Union County Health Department

Ryan Thornburg, Carolina Data Desk

February 16, 2021

Union County is served by the Union County Health Department. From 2010 to 2018 the department’s spending fell by 22%, when adjusted for inflation. During that same time, the population served by the Union County Health Department rose by 15%. When taking that population change into account, the per-person spending fell 32%.

In 2010, the Union County Health Department ranked 5 in per-person spending among the 46 health departments for which we have complete data. In 2018, it ranked 1.

Across the state, county-level spending on public health dropped 22% from 2010-2018 when adjusted for inflation and population increase.
